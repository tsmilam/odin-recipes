Ingredients:

  • ground beef
  • crushed tomatoes
  • tomato paste
  • bay leaf
  • bell pepper
  • 1 whole diced onion
  • 3 - 6 cloves of garlic
  • oregano
  • italian seasoning
  • basil

Steps:

  1. Brown the ground beef until mostly done and very little pink. Strain fat with colander.
  2. Add brown beef back to skillet with onion on medium heat.
  3. Once the onion has become cooked soft and translucent, add all of the spices along with garlic and cook for 3 more minutes.
  4. Transfer everything to a pot and add the crushed tomatoes with tomato paste.
  5. Simmer for at least 4 hours, stirring occassionally so that the sauce doesn't stick to the bottom of the pan.
